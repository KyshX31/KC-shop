How To Style a Quiff

Styling a quiff is all about achieving a balance between classic sophistication and modern flair. To create this iconic hairstyle, start with freshly washed and dried hair. Apply a good-quality pomade or styling product to add volume and hold. Use a comb to gently tease the hair on top of your head, lifting it upwards and slightly towards the back. This will create the signature height and shape of the quiff. Then, use a blow dryer on low heat to set the style, while continuing to shape the quiff with your comb. Finish with a touch of hairspray for added hold and shine. Remember, the key to a perfect quiff is practice, so don't be discouraged if it takes a few tries to get it just right.
